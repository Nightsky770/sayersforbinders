\begin{minipage}{\textwidth}
\textit{Clouds of Witness} was Dorothy L. Sayers' (1893\textendash1957) second Lord~Peter Wimsey novel. It was originally published in 1926 by T. Fisher Unwin in London (UK).
\end{minipage}
